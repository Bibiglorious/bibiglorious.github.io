Sentiment Analysis of Amazon Video Game Reviews

Evaluating Machine Learning Algorithms and Identifying Successful Companies

In this project, we applied Natural Language Processing techniques to perform sentiment analysis on customer reviews of Android applications. The text data underwent preprocessing steps such as tokenization, lemmatization, stopword removal, and special character filtering. We transformed the processed text into numerical representations using Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF) and employed machine learning models (kNN and SVM) to classify sentiment into negative, neutral, and positive categories
